1. Project Overview

StoryGrid Media is a digital media and content production agency that helps creators, founders, and companies scale their online presence through structured content systems.

The core idea behind the brand:

Story → narrative and personal brand

Grid → structured content systems

Media → digital platforms like YouTube, Instagram, podcasts

Positioning philosophy:

StoryGrid Media builds structured content systems that help creators grow consistently rather than chasing virality.

The company aims to be positioned not as a typical editing agency but as a content systems partner.

3. Website Strategy

The StoryGrid Media website is designed to function as a conversion-focused agency landing page.

Primary goal:

Capture inbound leads from creators, founders, and companies looking for content production and management.

Pricing will not be shown on the website.

Clients must fill a form or book a call.
